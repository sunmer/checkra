Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-04-16

At Checkra.io, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect and Use

AI Model Training

We value your data privacy. We explicitly do not use the prompts, images, or website content you submit through Checkra to train our AI models or any third-party AI models.

Data Storage

We do not store the prompts you enter or the images generated from your website selections on our servers after the feedback request is processed. The data is sent to the AI provider for analysis and then discarded by us.

Analytics

We use analytics tools (like Plausible Analytics or similar privacy-focused services) to understand how our service is used. This helps us improve Checkra. The data collected is aggregated and anonymized, focusing on usage patterns rather than individual user identification. We track things like button clicks, feature usage frequency, and general website interaction metrics.

Third-Party Services

Checkra utilizes third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude) to generate feedback. Your prompts and relevant website data (HTML snippets, screenshots) are sent to these providers solely for the purpose of generating the requested feedback. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers as well.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
